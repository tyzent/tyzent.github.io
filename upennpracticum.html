Practicum goals

  • Develop a personal relationship with a mentor The student would be connected with me, the CEO of Chariots for Hope. I would be the primary contact and able to meet with the student on an ongoing basis. We can discuss the challenges that have faced Chariots for Hope over the past 13 years. Would be able to work alongside our leadership team, including our Program Director, Communications Manager, and Kenyan leadership. If you are interested in an opportunity to learn cross-culturally, this might be a good fit for you. You could sit in on strategic and tactical meetings held by the leadership and management teams and work alongside board members at events. We can discuss other similar non-profits, and how Chariots for Hope differs from them in our US and Kenyan structure.
  • Exposure to organizational workings In addition to being a participant observer in the leadership and management meetings, the student will be involved in conversations around budget development, development strategy, partnership development, and fundraising events.
  • Potential project work
    • Creating a draft employee handbook
    • Event planning
    • Strategic planning
    • Development strategy
    • Help develop an alumni network
    • Help develop an education fund
    • Develop on-boarding strategy for US Employees
    • Develop on-boarding strategy for board members
    • Assess sustainable farming and agriculture projects
    • Create strategy for training children and staff on computers
    • Draft partnership agreement and expectations
    • Develop grant strategy
    • Improving financial accountability and reporting
