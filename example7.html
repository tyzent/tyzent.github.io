Chariots for Hope

12/31/2025 Strategic Plan

Build strong leadership, management, and home teams

  • Develop onboarding process, implement US employee handbook, and refine needed HR policies and procedures
  • Hire Development Director and Office Manager/Administrative Assistant
  • All staff are able to reside on campus in adequate accommodations, and receive appropriate salary & benefits
  • CFH is a registered legal entity in Kenya, with strong in-country partnerships

Metrics and Milestones: By the end of 2023 the leadership and management teams are in place and the board has approved the onboarding process, employee handbook, and policy & procedural changes

Acquire committed funding for the full budget

  • Promote fully sustainable home projects
  • Determine plan for Mvita Farm and implement strategy
  • Grow funding partners and set clear expectation for givers and partners
  • Grants provide 5% of total revenue
  • Develop and implement programmatic support for guardians, communities, and alumni
  • Provide holistic care for the children in our program

Metrics and Milestones: By the end of 2022 Chariots has applied for 3 grants and the leadership team has approved a business plan for Mvita farm. By the end of 2023 Chariots has led vision trips for leaders of 3 prospective partnering entities

Develop clear education strategy

  • Improve educational foundation for children in our program
  • Determine plan for Chariots for Hope Academy at Maai Mahiu and implement strategy
  • Raise and invest $2M for education fund

Metrics and Milestones: By the end of 2022 the leadership team has approved a long term strategy for the Chariots for Hope Academy at Maai Mahiu and evaluated & agreed on potential partnership with Freedom Global. By the end of 2023 Chariots will have implemented an education fund committee and approved plans for a public launch

Continue board development

  • Develop onboarding process, clear expectations, and procedures
  • Conduct annual performance review of CEO with appropriate feedback and resources to fulfill job description

Metrics and Milestones: By June 2023, new processes are in place
